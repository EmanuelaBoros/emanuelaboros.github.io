Hello! I am Emanuela Boros, a Research Scientist based in Écublens, Vaud, Switzerland. I specialize in deep learning, natural language processing (NLP), and machine learning (ML). My professional journey has taken me through various esteemed institutions and roles, contributing significantly to the fields of AI and computational research.

## About Me

I currently work at DHLAB-EPFL, focusing on media monitoring of the past. Previously, I have been a research engineer at La Rochelle University, a machine learning scientist at Teklia, and a research engineer at LAL Orsay. I completed my PhD at CEA and Saclay University (Paris-Sud XI) where I focused on neural methods for event extraction from text.
