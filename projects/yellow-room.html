Yellow Room

2014 5 images recent photo collection

An interior sequence where colour, light, objects, and a room begin to behave like characters. A yellow room, apparently. Sometimes that is enough trouble.

Artist note

What this was, unfortunately

An exercise in anti-romanticism. A mirror, a camera, a cat.

I couldn’t go outside, so I turned the room into the only world I had. The mirror made it look larger, but it only made me feel smaller.

No myth, no beauty — just a body in a yellow box, staring back at itself while the cat passed through, completely indifferent.
