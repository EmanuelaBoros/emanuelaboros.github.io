Space in Time

2010 6 portfolio plates from the 2010 portfolio

Space, unlike time, can be reused. Private space, as the primary reason of this project, appears in both its concept and its place in the exhibition space. How the private hurts us so deeply because it touches our essential core wound, it also shows our wild mortality. Interactivity between viewer and artist creates a distress motivating different reactions from various persons and processes of reflection / self-reflection.
