Christmas '10

2010 4 portfolio plates from the 2010 portfolio

The project is a compilation of all the events from 2010 Christmas. It also involves the familiar surroundings and the maybe unnoticed happenings around us. The most important event was celebrating Christmas, but this also has lots of small moments that build the period in time, forming the loop of time in our memory.
